## ADDENDUM â€” Progressive Player Intelligence
*Daniel's long-term profiling concept (20+ game window)*

### Concept
The app should track **where and how** a player is most effective over time, building a
spatial and contextual intelligence profile that goes beyond per-match scoring.

### Core Idea
Rather than just measuring *whether* a player can do something, the system identifies
*their best version* of each skill and amplifies it through coaching prompts.

### Examples
- "Channel runs down the left = 40% more effective than right â†’ develop left side further,
  OR prioritise learning right side depending on development goal"
- "Player escapes tight spaces well â†’ coach them to immediately drive forward or combine
  rather than defaulting to safe sideways pass after escape"
- "Shot selection: 68% accuracy from central positions, 31% from wide â†’ reinforce central
  finishing, develop wide-angle decision-making"

### What the System Tracks (Long-Term)
| Dimension | What's Tracked |
|---|---|
| Spatial effectiveness | Which zones / channels the player performs best in |
| Strength amplification | What they do well â†’ build coaching cues to maximise it |
| Weakness mapping | Consistent patterns of failure â†’ targeted development prompts |
| Situational patterns | Performs under pressure / drops off / inconsistent in transition |
| Skill ceiling signals | Sub-sub scores plateauing â†’ player approaching ceiling in that area |
| Context performance | Better in certain formations / against certain press styles? |

### Implementation Notes
