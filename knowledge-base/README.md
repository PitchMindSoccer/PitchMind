### 📚 COACHING CONCEPTS (6 files)
Core tactical and technical coaching knowledge

1. **[Pressing Theory](coaching/pressing-theory.md)**
   - Understand pressing structures, triggers, and collective coordination
   - Case studies from Serie A (Bologna, Como, Inter, Milan, Lazio)
   - Data-driven pressing analysis using StatsBomb pressure metrics
   - Keywords: *high press, low block, trigger-based, collective timing, press resistance*

2. **[Space Management & Positional Control](coaching/space-management.md)**
   - How elite teams control the space opponents operate in
   - Five defensive categories and their characteristics
   - Depth management and step-up mechanics
   - Keywords: *space conceded, compactness, step-up, mid-block, risk/control balance*

3. **[Ball Progression: Passing vs. Carrying](coaching/ball-progression.md)**
   - Strategic decision-making between passing and dribbling
   - League-by-league progression styles (Bundesliga, Ligue 1, Premier League, La Liga)
   - Team case studies: Arsenal, PSG, Brentford, Fulham
   - Keywords: *progressive pass, carry, risk assessment, context-dependent, efficiency*

4. **[Quick Passing & First Touch Execution](coaching/quick-passing-first-touch.md)**
   - The art of receiving and immediately releasing the ball (<1 second)
   - Head-up scanning and anticipation
   - Elite quick passers: Rodri, Jorginho, Harvey Elliott
   - Keywords: *quick pass, first touch, scanning, OBV, press resistance, decision speed*

5. **[First Touch Fundamentals](coaching/first-touch-fundamentals.md)**
   - Technical foundation for all soccer skills
   - Touch types: inside foot, outside foot, sole, cushioning
   - Age-appropriate progression (U8-U14+)
   - Keywords: *touch quality, orientation, body position, both-feet, pressure adaptation*

6. **[Off-Ball Movement & Positioning](coaching/off-ball-movement.md)**
   - How players position and move WITHOUT the ball
   - Support principles, width/depth management, run timing
   - Position-specific requirements (fullback, center-back, midfielder, forward)
   - Keywords: *positioning, run timing, support angles, shape maintenance, spacing*
