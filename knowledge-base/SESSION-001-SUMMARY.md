## Key Insights & Themes Across Files

### 1. **Pressing is Nuanced**
- Not just "high press vs. low block"
- Modern pressing includes phased approaches (Bologna: continuous, Inter: high then drop, Lazio: trigger-based)
- Pressure data shows space conceded, not just defensive actions
- Pressing effectiveness ties to collective structure, not individual effort

### 2. **Space Management is Central**
- Everything in modern soccer revolves around space
- Space is created through player positioning (width, depth, variation)
- Space is consumed through pressing (reducing distance to ball receiver)
- Space is utilized through movement timing (arriving into space at right moment)
- Data quantification: average distance to nearest defender by zone

### 3. **Decision-Making Hierarchy**
- Under time pressure, players follow a priority order
- Best option first (forward pass) → safety option (backward pass)
- Context matters (score situation, opponent quality, match phase)
- Time constraint is real: <1-2 seconds under pressure

### 4. **First Touch is Foundation**
- All subsequent actions depend on quality first touch
- Touch direction is as important as touch quality
- Body orientation while receiving determines available next actions
- Both-foot competency is non-negotiable at elite level

### 5. **Off-Ball Movement Separates Elite from Average**
- Elite teams: 9-10 players moving intelligently
- Average teams: Most players stationary
- Support principle ensures ball carrier always has options
- Movement timing is harder to coach than first touch

### 6. **Flexibility Over Rigidity**
- Top teams mix passing and carrying (not all-pass or all-carry)
- Defensive approaches vary by zone and match context
- Same player makes different decisions based on space available
- System > individual skill (though individual skill executes system)

### 7. **Data Enables Precision Coaching**
- StatsBomb pressure, OBV, xPass metrics quantify previously subjective areas
- Heat maps show team defensive philosophy
- Player-level data identifies strengths/weaknesses
- Video analysis confirms and explains data findings
