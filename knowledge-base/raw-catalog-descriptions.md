## One-to-One: Jez Weeks — Dribbling with Decision-Making
**URL:** /programs/jez-weeks-dribbling-with-decision-making
**Videos:** 9 | **Format:** Warm-up + Practice 1 + Practice 2 + Coach Review

Grassroots coaching specialist Jez Weeks takes players through three practices designed to develop their ability to make decisions WHILE dribbling. The session is divided into a warm-up, a semi-opposed technical practice, and a conditioned game. All three practices are set up in an identical playing area featuring six triangles, presenting players with various challenges throughout. Accompanied by PDF session plan.

**Key structure:**
- Warm-up (14:25) — Triangle-based decision-making
- Practice 1 (13:56) — Semi-opposed dribbling choices
- Practice 2 (12:43) — Conditioned game with decision triggers
- Coach Review (4:20)
