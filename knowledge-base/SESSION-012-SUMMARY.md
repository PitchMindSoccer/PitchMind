### Critical Scoring Notes

**CM Position Specificity:**
- **CMs are balanced defenders** — they must maintain positional discipline and read opposition movement pre-pass
- **CMs are balanced attackers** — they make forward runs, but not with the frequency or directness of CAMs
- **Leo must penalize CMs for defensive lapses** — bunching with CDM, losing position after attacking, failing to intercept
- **Leo must reward CMs for tempo control** — managing the speed of play, knowing when to accelerate or slow the attack

**CAM Position Specificity:**
- **CAMs are pure attackers** — their job is to create and score; defensive responsibility is secondary
- **CAMs are decision-makers** — every touch is a decision (shoot, pass, dribble, recycle)
- **Leo must heavily reward CAMs for chance creation** — assists, key passes, and 1v1 situations created
- **Leo must penalize CAMs for turnovers in dangerous areas** — the opposition counter-attack is the CAM's biggest risk
- **CAM's shooting is position-specific** — reward for difficult shots that are on target; harsher penalty for difficult shots that miss
